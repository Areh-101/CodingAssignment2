Delicious Ravioli Recipe

Ravioli

Recipe by Arehone Rahulani (original creation)

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour.
  • 2 large eggs.
  • 1 cup ricotta cheese.
  • 1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese.
  • Salt and pepper to taste.
  • Olive oil for serving.

Equipment

  • Mixing bowl.
  • Rolling pin.
  • Ravioli cutter or knife.
  • Pot for boiling.
  • Serving plate.

Directions

  1. Mix flour and eggs to form a dough. Knead until smooth.
  2. Roll out dough into thin sheets.
  3. Place small spoonfuls of ricotta mixture onto one sheet.
  4. Cover with another sheet and press edges to seal ravioli.
  5. Boil in salted water until they float, about 3-4 minutes.
  6. Serve with olive oil and sprinkle with Parmesan cheese.
